The Nall's Story

The American dream,
one mile at a time.

Every successful company starts with a dream. For Nall's Specialized Hauling, that dream began in 1996 with one truck and one determined man, Charles Nall.

Charles did not come from wealth or privilege. Raised in a working family, he learned early that nothing worth having comes easy. His father taught him that success is earned through hard work, dedication, honesty, and perseverance. His mother gave him another lesson that would shape both his life and the culture of his company: treat the janitor with the same respect you give the CEO, because every person deserves dignity and kindness.

With those values as his foundation, and with the help of his brother Calvin, Charles took a leap of faith and started Nall's Specialized Hauling with a single truck. Long days, sleepless nights, and countless miles became the norm. Every load was delivered with pride. Every customer was treated like family. Every challenge became another opportunity to prove what hard work could accomplish.

Over the years, that one truck grew into a fleet of 125 power units supported by 400 trailers. Nall's now serves local, regional, and over-the-road customers throughout the lower 48 states with the same commitment to safety, reliability, and integrity that guided the company from the beginning.

Today, Nall's Specialized Hauling stands as a testament to what can be built without shortcuts. The fleet has grown and the loads have become more complex, but the values remain the same: work hard, treat people right, keep your word, and deliver with pride.
